Work Experience

Here's my work experience, and experience in miscellaneous projects during my undergrad.

Paid Research Intern

LUAR Laboratory @ UFMG - DCC

June 2018 - December 2018

Scholarship at the PASARP project, financed by EMBRAER and FAPEMIG, dealing as a consulting intern in the field of aeronautic engineering and conducting research in control schemes for aircraft.

The aim of the project was automatic verification of aircraft system software. The scholarship was paid through the sponsor financing.

Paid Development Intern

Invent Vision

June 2021 - June 2022

Hired as a Software Development intern, making use of C++ in the Qt Framework and the OpenCV library for the development of industrial inspection applications to be deployed for clients of the company.

Key tasks:

  • Made use of OpenCV and C++ to implement a dimmensional inspection functionality for an automotive spring manufacturer.
  • Implementation of various UI elements, and functionalities for the company's main inspection plataform, Inspecta, utilizing C++ on the Qt Framework.
  • Utilization of Pandas in Python for data visualization of datasets related to machine learning projects' presentation.
  • Improvement of scrapping script with Python for image gathering, followed by manual annotation for the purposes of the machine learning necessities of the company.

Software Team Leader

Student Initiative AVANT - UFMG

January 2018 - December 2020

Lead the Software & Electronics Team in developing subroutines and algorithms for use in small UAVs developed by AVANT - UFMG.

Key contributions:

  • Responsible for setting the software development pipeline and implementing an Agile system.
  • Developing and coordinating the development of computer vision routines in OpenCV for the detection of objectives in the AUVSI SUAS competition.
  • Integrating the needs of the AVANT team with the development schedule of the Software Team.
  • Training and coordinating with the multiple sections of the Software and Electronics Team for the implementation of the auto-pilot-flight-computer-actuators system that goes within the UAVs.
  • General planning and administration of opportunities for collaboration and application of the team's resources with various external entities
